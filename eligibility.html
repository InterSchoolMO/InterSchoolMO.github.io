Eligibility

ISMO is open to every student who:

  • Is enrolled at one of the participating schools.
  • Is not a member of the Jury, the Board, or the markers.
  • Has not proposed a problem to the ISMO between the start of the previous contest and the start of the current contest.

Contestants are divided by year groups into the middle school division (7-8), the junior division (9-11), and the senior division (12-13).

Past members of the New Zealand Math Olympiad January camp can participate but are discounted from the official ranking.

Registration should be completed through your school. If your school is not one of the participating schools, you can ask your math department to join us, or become an organiser yourself.
